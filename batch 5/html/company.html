About Purwadhika

Purwadhika have delivered training sessions to professionals as well as public governmental and private companies, both national and international such as Astra International, Astra Honda Motor, Freeport Indonesia, Total Indonesie, Bank Central Asia, Citibank, TNI AL (The Indonesian Navy), DPR RI (The People’s Representatives Council), Lembaga Sandi Nasional (National Crypto Agency), American Embassy, Pertamina, Bukit Asam Coal Mining, Esso Oil, and many more.

What makes Purwadhika different is how we combine education and incubation into one perfect entity to equip you in building a successful Startup. I believe that in order for a Startup to be successful, it must have a strong foundation which is a relevant education, with the finishing touch in the right incubator to create qualified products. Trust us, your decision to join us will be one of the best decisions you’ve ever made in your entire life.
